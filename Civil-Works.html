Civil Works

Construction

For civil work projects, precision and efficiency are crucial in construction projects. That's why we utilize the latest and most advanced technology, including our Total Station TS16, to tailor our measurement and working methods to meet the specific needs and preferences of our clients. We use the RTC360 3D laser scanner (see specific page) to quickly and precisely capture data on complex structures. This technology enables us to create detailed BIM models for better planning and management of construction projects.

Different types of surveys in this area are:

  • Dimensional surveys
  • As-built surveys
  • GPS surveys
  • 3D-deformation measurements
  • Infrastructure
  • Mass calculations - documentation of roads, landscapes building and structures
  • Terrain profiles - cross section
  • Setting out piles and foundations
  • Civil Engineering Structures (bridges)
  • Out-of-court expertise (with real-time monitoring and geo-positioning systems)
In construction projects it is always very important to adjust the measuring method (accuracy) and working method to the wishes of the customer and achieving high efficiency. We mainly use hereby our total stations TS16, but sometimes also GPS and/or scanner RTC360 (see specific page)
LEICA RTC360 high speed laser
  • Range: min. 0.5m up to 130m
  • Measurement rate: up to 2million points per second
  • Laser class: 1
  • Integrated color camera: yes
  • Operating temperature: -5 °C to +40 °C

3D scanning and BIM

3D scanning and modeling can be an interesting option for renovations, MEP design (mechanical, electrical and plumbing) and information within a building information management (BIM)
Learn More
We prefer to combine scanning with total station measurements of reference points and critical control points to achieve higher accuracy. The end result (a cleaned-up point cloud) can be modeled according to the wishes of the customer via suitable software such as Bricscad BIM, cloudworx, Revit,etc. This is labour-intensive drawing work that is often outsourced by various agencies, but that we also carry out in-house.

LEICA TS 30 high speed laser

  • Range: min. 0.5m up to 130m
  • Measurement rate: up to 2million points per second
  • Laser class: 1
  • Integrated color camera: yes
  • Operating temperature: -5 °C to +40 °C
Fast
The Leica RTC360 laser scanner makes 3D reality capture faster than ever before. With a measuring rate of up to 2 million points per second and advanced HDR imaging system, the creation of coloured 3D point clouds can be completed in under 2 minutes. Plus, automated targetless field registration (based on VIS technology) and the seamless, automated transfer of data from site to office reduce time spent in the field and further maximise productivity.
Agile
Small and lightweight, the Leica RTC360 scanner's portable design and collapsible tripod mean it's compact enough to fit into most backpacks, ready to be taken anywhere. Once on-site, easy-to-use one-button operation makes for fast, hassle-free scanning.
Precise
Low noise data allows for better images, resulting in crisp, high-quality scans that are rich in detail and ready for use in a range of applications. Combined with Cyclone FIELD 360 software for automated registration in the field, the Leica RTC360 scanner offers outstanding precision that can be checked on-site.
Laser scanning in industrial & civil works allows precise measurements and serves as a cost- and time-saving device while ensuring a high level of quality.
